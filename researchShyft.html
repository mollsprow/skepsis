Some initial gleaning...

The Team @ Shyft
  • MyCommunitySite is in need of a UX facelift
  • Shyft, though mighty, is small in resources.
  • Shyft has a list of issues they are aware of.
  • Improving the usability of their website will save both Shyft and clients time.
MyCommunitySite Users
  • Are happy with MyCommunitySite, namely the customer service.
  • 7/8 of survey respondents pay for the site to be managed by Shyft.
  • All respondants at have reached out to Shyft for help with their site.
  • Most consider themselves tech savvy.
Our Game Plan
  • Pinpoint areas of the site that suffer the most from bad user experience.
  • Design and research ways to improve those areas, without losing the integrity of existing features.
  • Keep Shyft's company size and resources in mind at all times.
  • Don't fix what isn't broken
